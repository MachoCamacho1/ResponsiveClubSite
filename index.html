Who Are We?

We are a dedicated group of hikers all over the country that love to challenge ourselves to hike the easiest and meanest trail the country has to offer.

As a group, we also share our equipment by renting it to each other. We believe that seperates us from other hiking groups.

Join Us

If you are a dedicated hiker as us, please JOIN US. We plan together, we travel together and we achieve our goals together. No man or woman gets left behind!
